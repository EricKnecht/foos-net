Welcome to FoosNet

Bringing competition to the game. Ready to get started?

‹ ›

1. Sign up

First things first, you have to register as a player. Player registration is free and all it requires is a name and valid email. Sign up to get started!

2. Join a league

Join a league and see how you rate up against other players. Some leagues are tougher than others, so make sure you know what you are getting into!

... or start one

Do you play with a group of players often? You can start your own league to get the most accurate ratings for your group!

3. Record matches

Matches are played in pairs, but players are scored individually on an Elo rating system. Get into a league with people you know, to start recording matches!
