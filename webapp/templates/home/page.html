Start a league

Matches are played in leagues. Start a league with people you play with often and see how you rate up against each other!

Join a league

You can join multiple leagues and carry a different rating in each league. Some leagues are tougher than others, so make sure you know what you are getting into!

Record a game

Games are played in pairs, but players are scored individually. Join an existing league, or start your own, to start playing games!
